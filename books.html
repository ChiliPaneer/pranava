Oka Amma Akorsam

This short novel represents a family’s agony, when the older son is diagnosed with mental health challenges. Born & raised as a very intelligent & caring individual , the mental health changes rock the family’s integrity , financial & physical resources. This is written to bring awareness to the highly stigmatized mental health issues in the community, & to remind that illness happens- does not have to be someone’s fault.
